# Content Templates

Scope: Copy templates for placeholder and early parity screens.

## Placeholder Subtitle Template

Use:

`Phase {N} placeholder: {feature outcome statement}.`

Examples:
- `Phase 2 placeholder: Lead capture, pipeline updates, and status actions land here.`
- `Phase 4 placeholder: Job phase tracking and detail controls land here.`

## Empty State Template

Use:

- Title: `{Entity} not found` or `No {entities} yet`
- Body: `Add your first {entity} to get started.`
- CTA: `Add {Entity}` or `Go to {primary screen}`

## Confirmation Copy Template

Use:

- Title: `{Action} {record_name}?`
- Body: `{Consequence sentence}.`
- Confirm CTA: `{Action}`
- Cancel CTA: `Cancel`

Examples:
- `Stop all follow-ups for John Smith?`
- `Delete Maria from your leads?`

## Primary CTA Label Template

Use short verb-first labels:
- `Save Lead`
- `Save Client`
- `Set Up Project`
- `Mark as Won`
- `Estimate Sent`
- `Save as Lead`

## Route-Linked Header Template

Use:

- `/leads` -> `Leads`
- `/lead-capture` -> `New Lead`
- `/projects/new` -> `New Project`
- `/onboarding` -> `Import Data`

## Phase Tagging Template

For parity scaffolds, append one line in subtitle or body:

`Phase owner: [BIZ-COFOUNDER] | Implementation handoff pending.`
